About LeBlanc Skate Shop

LeBlanc Skate Shop is owned and operated by Marc LeBlanc, who has been taking care of skates at the Libro Centre in Amherstburg since 2015. Hockey skates, goalie skates, fingure skates, sledgehockey blades... Marc's just about seen it all!

LeBlanc Skate Shop is more than just skate sharpening! See Marc for laces, tape, mouthguards, skateguards, water bottles, names bars, and lots more

Services & Products

Sharpening & Profiling

Sharpening and profiling for hockey skates, goalie skate, figure skates, and sledge blades

Accessories

Laces, stick and sock tape, skate guards, mouth guards, water bottles, and so much more

Precision Guarantee

Your blades are checked and then checked again to make sure your edges are nice and sharp

Team Apparel

Jersey namebars, t-shirts, sweatshirts, polos... Marc has apparel to meet your needs
